Current Work

Avaliable for Hire. Currently working on my own personal project. Using real world terrain data to duplicate real world nature walks within Unreal Engine. The game is a relaxing walking simulator game that creates a visualisation of depression and a replication of what they may be experiencing, such as negative thought patterns, colourless worlds, and how hard simple decisions can become. I am also creating a visualisation of how their mood can shape their perspective of the world. It also explores the restorative impact of exploring nature and how exercise can benefit mental and physical health.
