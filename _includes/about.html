About

Gameplay focused developer with industry experience in both the Unity Engine and the Unreal Engine, specializing in creating performant, polished, and engaging gameplay mechanics for players and developers alike. I have contributed a vast variety of projects from PlanetSmith (Incandescient Games) - an open world game based on hex-grid voxel style spherical planet, to Axon (Exsurgo) - a revolutionary new device to help chronic pain sufferers, and Wanderer (Mighty Eyes) - a time travel VR game. Where I contributed to unique gameplay systems and refined existing systems to create the most seamless and immersive experience possible.

Holding a Bachelor of Software Engineering from Media Design School, I have worked collaboratively in multiple different teams and team sizes to create works of art that I can be proud of. My hands-on experience working with Unity ECS, Unity Jobs, enhancing and extending custom Unity editor tools, has allowed me to deliver high quality gameplay features that have been well-received by both players and developers alike.
